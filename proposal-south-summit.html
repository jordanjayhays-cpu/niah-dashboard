Section 01

The Problem & The Solution

Why networking fails at after-party scale — and how Niah fixes it

The Problem

At 50–150 people in one room, meaningful connections are still hard to find. Networking becomes noise.

  • No intelligent matching — just proximity and chance
  • Investor time is expensive — bad meetings waste it
  • Startups can't find the right investors fast enough
  • Corporates struggle to identify relevant innovators
  • Post-event follow-up is manual and inconsistent

Our Solution

Niah learns each attendee's profile and delivers daily curated match recommendations.

  • AI matching: investor thesis ↔ startup stage/market
  • Corporate needs ↔ solution providers on the floor
  • Conversation openers included — not just names
  • Meeting requests with rationales sent directly
  • 30-day follow-up reminders post-event
1

Why This Works for South Summit After Party

The after-party brings together investors × startups × corporates in one room — the ideal matchmaking geometry. Niah uses a short intake survey to learn each attendee's profile and delivers match recommendations before they even arrive.

Pilot risk to South Summit is zero: Niah operates at no cost in exchange for physical space, email promotion, branding placement, and a post-event case study. We fund our own tools and infrastructure.
