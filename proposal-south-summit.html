South Summit Madrid (June 3–5, 2026) brings together 20,000 attendees, 2,100 investors, and 134 countries around a shared mission: connecting entrepreneurs with capital, corporates, and each other. That mission depends entirely on one thing — quality networking.

Yet at this scale, meaningful connection is nearly impossible. Attendees rely on random encounters, badge scanning, and LinkedIn scraping — producing volume, not quality. Niah solves this: an AI matchmaking tool that learns attendee profiles and delivers 3–5 curated introductions per day, with meeting rationale and conversation openers.

This proposal outlines a zero-cost pilot partnership in exchange for branding placement and a post-event case study. It also details the tool infrastructure costs we are asking South Summit to sponsor, which directly enable Niah to operate at South Summit scale.

Section 01

The Problem & The Solution

Why networking fails at South Summit scale — and how Niah fixes it

The Problem

At 20,000 people, the odds of a valuable, serendipitous meeting are near zero. Networking becomes noise.

  • No intelligent matching — just proximity and chance
  • Investor time is expensive — bad meetings waste it
  • Startups can't find the right investors fast enough
  • Corporates struggle to identify relevant innovators
  • Post-event follow-up is manual and inconsistent

Our Solution

Niah learns each attendee's profile and delivers daily curated match recommendations.

  • AI matching: investor thesis ↔ startup stage/market
  • Corporate needs ↔ solution providers on the floor
  • Conversation openers included — not just names
  • Meeting requests with rationales sent directly
  • 30-day follow-up reminders post-event
1

Why This Works for South Summit

South Summit's three-way ecosystem — investors × startups × corporations — is the ideal matchmaking geometry. The matching logic is already embedded in attendee registration categories. Niah doesn't need to guess; it works with the data South Summit already collects.

Pilot risk to South Summit is zero: Niah operates at no cost in exchange for branding placement and post-event feedback. We fund our own tools and infrastructure. If results don't meet expectations, there is no obligation to continue.
